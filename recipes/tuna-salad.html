Tuna Salad

Description

This is a recipe for a delicious tuna salad, perfect for a warm day with some crackers, tostadas, or even on a sandwich.

This recipe uses a perfect harmony of tuna, mayo, and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Tuna in a can
  • 2 Tbsp of mayo
  • Mixed vegetables

Steps

  1. Put tuna in a bowl with the mayo
  2. Add vegetables
  3. Mix and Enjoy!
