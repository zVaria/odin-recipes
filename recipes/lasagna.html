Lasagna

Description

This is a lasagna recipe that has been passed down for generation and generation. (not really it's a random recipe) Make with the freshest ingredients possible like noodles, tomatoes, ricotta.

Even use the hottest oven possible. Any less and you tarnish the family name.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • Noodles
  • Ricotta
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Brown meat with a bit of olive oil in pan
  2. Add onions and garlic and cook down
  3. Once everything is cooked add tomato sauce and let simmer 30 min
  4. Lay noodles in baking tray and cover with ricotta and sauce, repeat until desirable lasagna stack height
  5. Put this in the oven at 450 for 30 minutes
